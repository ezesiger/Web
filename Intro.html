This website is about surveillance. Since I was 14 years old I have been using Google Keep as my notes app. For 7 years I have been writing everything from passwords, to lists, to dreams, to diary entries in Google Keep. This website contains 40 of those notes.

I wanted to explore the idea of surveillance in this project. What does it mean that Google has all this information about me. The most intimate details of my life are stored somewhere in the Google cloud server. Is it being used to train AI or sell me ads? Can corporations and governments access this information without my knowledge? Google has faced lawsuit after lawsuit over privacy violations.

I wanted to put you in the position of the surveiller. What can you find out about me from reading through this selection of notes? If these are the notes I included what were in the ones that I left out?
