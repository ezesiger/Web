What is a meme?

Originally: referred to ideas or behaviors that spread culturally through person to person imitation

Now: refers to internet memes, often formulaic and easy to make and edit. Memes typically feature absurdist text on or above a low-res image

They are still shared from person to person and are often remixed and reinterpreted en-masse leading to templates or trends. The way they travel from person to person highlights how information spreads on the internet. Through social media, downloads, direct message, and forums
